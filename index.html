About Me

I am a graduate student in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at KAIST, working with Ilmun Kim. I transferred to KAIST from the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Yonsei University, where I previously studied as a graduate student.

My research interests include hypothesis testing, kernel methods, conformal inference, and distribution-free statistical inference.
